About Our Organization

Pioneering the future of space exploration, research, and education since 2008.

Who We Are

The Cosmic Exploration Initiative (CEI) is an international non-profit organization dedicated to advancing humanity's understanding and presence in space. Founded in 2008 by a team of visionary scientists, engineers, and educators, CEI has grown to become a leading force in space research, technology development, and public outreach.

With our headquarters in Geneva, Switzerland, and research campuses across four continents, we bring together the brightest minds from around the globe to tackle the most challenging questions about our universe.

"To inspire, explore, and understand the cosmos, making space accessible for all humanity while fostering a deeper connection between Earth and the universe we inhabit."

Our Emblem

Our emblem represents humanity's journey into space. The central orbit symbolizes our connection to Earth while reaching outward. The seven stars represent our founding principles: Exploration, Innovation, Education, Sustainability, Collaboration, Ethics, and Wonder.

Global Presence

Our international campuses serve as centers of excellence for space research and education:

  • Pasadena, California, USA
  • Bangalore, India

Partnerships

  • Memorandum of Understanding with NASA

  • Research collaboration with MIT Space Lab
  • Academic affiliation with International Space University

Cosmic Perspective

At CEI, we recognize that space exploration transcends scientific achievement. Our work fosters a cosmic perspective - the understanding that we are part of something vastly greater than ourselves. This perspective cultivates humility, interconnectedness, and a profound sense of stewardship for our planet and species.

We encourage contemplation of our place in the universe, recognizing that the journey outward is simultaneously a journey inward. Our programs incorporate philosophical discussions, mindfulness practices, and ethical frameworks to ensure our exploration of space remains grounded in wisdom and reverence for the cosmos.
